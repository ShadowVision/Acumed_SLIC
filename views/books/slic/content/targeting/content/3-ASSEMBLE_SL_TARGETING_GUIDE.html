3 ASSEMBLE SL TARGETING GUIDE

The SL Targeting Guide will need to be assembled prior to utilization. Remove the SL Targeting Guide instrumentation from the SLIC Screw Prep Pack. Begin with the SL Targeting Guide Elevator Assembly (80-1060) on the sterile table and remove the SL Targeting Guide Dorsal Plate (80-1051) from the Elevator Assembly. Take the SL Targeting Guide Targeting Wing (80-1052) and thread it onto the screw at the bottom of the Elevator Assembly. Continue to turn the Elevator Screw until the Targeting Wing is about mid way up the Elevator Screw. The SL Targeting Guide is now ready to be positioned on the patient’s wrist.

Place the Elevator Assembly under the wrist against the volar surface of the wrist.

Note: The Elevator Assembly will have the most stability if the Elevator Post is flush to the radial side of the wrist.
