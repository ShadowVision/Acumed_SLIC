4 CONFIRMATION OF GUIDE WIRE POSITION

Confirm the orientation of the 0.045" Guide Wire in both the PA and lateral views. Make sure the 0.045" Guide Wire is centered in the scaphoid and lunate as seen in the lateral view or just volar of the midline. The correct location in the lateral view will be centered in the middle third of the lunate. Advance the guide wire across the wrist and out through the skin ulnarly. A second guide wire may be placed across the scaphoid and lunate to control rotation as the interval is drilled.

Tip: To ensure precise placement, on the lateral view line up the view so that the 0.045" Guide Wire appears as a point or a short line in the radiograph.
