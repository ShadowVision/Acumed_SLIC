7 INSERT THE SLIC SCREW DEVICE

Slowly advance the SLIC Screw device into the scaphoid, always keeping a positive insertion pressure on the SLIC Screw Driver to keep it engaged in the distal screw. Check the position of the screw under fluoroscopy every few turns of the SLIC Screw Driver or as necessary. Continue advancing until the step in the screw reaches the SL interval. Placing the step of the screw at the SL interval will allow toggle between the scaphoid and lunate. The driver and 0.045" Guide Wire can then be removed.
